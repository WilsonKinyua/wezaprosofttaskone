New Congress Dates

Due to the unprecedented nature of COVID-19 Pandemic, that has necessitated travel restrictions globally, the All Africa Youth Congress Secretariat, has made the difficult decision to postpone the congress that was set to take place in September 27 - October 02, Accra, Ghana. Planning still continues and a new date shall be announced soon.

About All Africa Youth Congress

The All Africa Youth Congress, the first of its kind, seeks to establish a movement of enthusiastic, creative and innovative young people, who are patriotic and inspired to harness their skills and talents for the transformation Africa.

Why the congress

We recognise the contribution of young people is paramount to the growth and future of the continent. All Africa Conference of Churches (AACC) therefore has taken the initiative to organize the 2020 maiden Congress whose theme is Africa: My Home. My Future.

Themes

Promoting African Patriotism

The youth congress will provide a space for reflection on Pan Africanism. It will bring different stakeholders, senior and youthful pan Africanists to critically engage on the needs of young people in Africa on economic, social and political front, reawakening the of pan Africanism among the youth.

Learn more...

Fostering active Citizenship

Africa’s young people have the potential, energy, numbers and talents to transform the continent’s fortunes. Empowering the youth to channel their efforts, intellect, energy for constructive outcomes will benefit both nations and the continent at large. The youth congress creates a space for engagement on how young people can become active citizens of their countries and communities.

Learn more...

Advocating for Justice and Security

To achieve the Agenda 2063, and to contribute towards agenda 2063 tackling issues of justice and security in Africa are important. The youth of Africa have the potential to champion gender justice, climate justice, to be ambassadors of peace, harmony and security. The youth congress is an opportunity to engage, organize, and advance the search for justice and security in Africa.

Learn more...

Campaigning for African Dignity

The ultimate goal for the Africa is to ensure peace, unity, solidarity, inclusion and dignity prevails on the continent. Reflecting on the liberation of Africa, and the achievements of post-independence Africa, young people need to take up the struggle of this day and era, which threaten the dignity and shared prosperity for Africa. The youth congress to reflect on issues of inequality, irregular migration, discrimination among other issues.
